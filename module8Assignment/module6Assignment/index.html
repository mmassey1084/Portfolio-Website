Different types of each cuisine

Classic Cheeseburger
A juicy beef patty topped with melted cheddar, lettuce, tomato, pickles, and onions, served on a toasted bun.
BBQ Bacon Burger
A savory burger featuring crispy bacon, cheddar cheese, onion rings, and a smoky BBQ sauce for a sweet and tangy twist.
Mushromm Swiss Buger
A rich combination of sautéed mushrooms, melted Swiss cheese, and garlic aioli, perfect for those who love earthy flavors.
Classic American Hot Dog
A grilled beef hot dog topped with ketchup, mustard, and relish, served on a soft bun.
Chili Cheese Dog
A hearty hot dog loaded with savory chili, melted cheddar cheese, and diced onions for an extra kick.
Chicago-Style Hot Dog
A beef hot dog dressed with mustard, onions, pickles, tomatoes, and sport peppers, all packed into a poppy seed bun for a fresh and tangy flavor.
Margherita Pizza
A simple yet delicious classic with fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and basil leaves, drizzled with olive oil on a thin crust.
Pepperoni Pizza
A fan favorite, topped with savory pepperoni slices and melted mozzarella cheese on a crispy, golden crust.
BBQ Chicken Pizza
A flavorful combination of BBQ sauce, grilled chicken, red onions, and cilantro, all on a perfectly baked crust.

Tandy Education Center

Historical Context

The Tandy Education Center, located on the OU-Tulsa campus, is named after the Tandy Foundation, known for its philanthropic contributions to education and healthcare in the Tulsa region. The center was established to support advanced learning, particularly in medical and healthcare education.

Current Usage

The Tandy Education Center is primarily used for medical education and training, hosting lectures, workshops, and continuing education programs. It serves both students and professionals in the healthcare fields, providing state-of-the-art facilities that support hands-on learning. The center is also used for seminars and events aimed at fostering professional development in medical and healthcare-related disciplines.

Architectural Details

The Tandy Education Center is a modern facility designed with both large lecture halls and smaller, interactive classroom spaces. Its sleek design emphasizes functionality, offering technologically advanced classrooms, including simulation labs and digital teaching tools. The building promotes a collaborative learning environment with flexible spaces that accommodate various teaching styles and formats.

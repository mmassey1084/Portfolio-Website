Founders Student Center

Historical Context

The Founders Student Center is dedicated to fostering student life and engagement on the OU-Tulsa campus. It serves as a central hub for student activities and community events.

Current Usage

Currently, the center is home to OU-Tulsa Student Affairs, where students can access services related to academics, well-being, and extracurricular involvement. It also houses meeting spaces for student organizations.

Architectural Details

This building has a welcoming, open-floor design with large common areas where students can relax and socialize. The architecture promotes a communal atmosphere with its modern design and flexible use of space.
